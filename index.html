Adventure

Are you looking for new adventures? We can help you explore Ohrid and his surroundings and book your next adventure.

Boat Trip

One day boat trip to fishermen village of Trpejca with few stops on the way. Swimming, relaxing, surfing, wild beach, beer, and excellent food in a restaurant by the water where you can taste the most amazing trout. We make sure for you to be on time for the trip and take you home to Paradise Nest again. All you need is your beach bag, smile on your face and lets do it attitude.

Hiking Trips

Hiking trips in the national park of Galichica with experienced guides. You'll be visiting rural villages and you will have the opportunity to see and feel how locals in those villages live. You get the opportunity to hear their stories, taste their delicious homemade sheep cheese and sing song or two with them. So grab your hiking boots, long pants, bottle of water and lets do it.

Scuba Diving

Explore the underwater world with professional scuba diver in the waters of Ohrid Lake. The whole experience takes around 3 hours. For beginners follows 45 minutes of introduction about the equipment and breathing techniques. Snacks and CD with photos from your adventure are included in the price. We take you and drive you back to Paradise Nest house.

Directions

Paradise Nest House is located 5km from Ohrid in village of Shipokno in the national park of Galichica. To get to us by car drive along Р1301/Р501 road or the road to St. Naum. After 3km on your left you'll see Villa Marta, so you have to turn left and when you get to villa Marta turn sharp right and drive up the mountain. There are few signs on the way to lead you to us. Please note that the road is not asphalted, since we are in the national park zone. If you have a hard time finding us, we'll be happy to pick you up, just write us an email. We can also arrange pick ups from the bus station and airport.
